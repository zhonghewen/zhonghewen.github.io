OVERVIEW

Problem Space

Main problems

  • The page is frozen after admin intiates a long action.
  • Other admin are unaware of the occupied resources and they could take actions which may cause conflicts.

The goal of SITCON is to support admin to initiate other actions and understand the potential conflicts.

Design Process

Outcomes

A long action center named SITCON where user can get access to new actions and monitor the progress of existing long actions.

  • Reliability

  • Scalability
