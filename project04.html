Overview

Introduction

There are some professional food delivery companies in China. However, customers complain a lot about their service: slow delivery, slow feedback and etc. There are also stake holders like the delivery man as well as the restaurant owner. This project aims to investigate the needs of each role and came up with a solution to solve the problem.

Duration

Sep.2015 - Oct. 2015

Process

01.INTERVIEW
Interviewed 3 delivery men, 2 customers, 2 restaurant owners.

02.AFFINITY MAPPING
Transcribed recordings to form affinity notes.

03.ANALYSIS
Present key insights, design oppotunities, main concepts.

04.SOLUTION
Explain the solutions and display example pages of app.

05.FLOW CHART
Made the flow chart of past and present services.
