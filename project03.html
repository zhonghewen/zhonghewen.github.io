1. Overview

Introduction

Camore is a star-up company which sells medicine on its app and provides 60-minute delivery service. My work here is to create the web pages of the management system based on the flow chart of ordering process. The management system includes functions like commodities management, customer service management, ordering management and etc. The ordering and customer service pages were translated into English while the others are not.

Duration

Jan.20 2015 - May.20 2016

My Role

  • Designed the web pages of the management system which is used for updating commodities, managing orders and etc.
  • Designed the mobile app pages for delivery men and designed the interaction between this mobile app and the management system.
  • Communicated with developers to make the expected effects of pages and functions come true.
  • Kept track of the debug lists and scheduled the debugging.

2. PROCESS

2.1 Study the flow chart and list the module needed.
2.2 Write the feature lists of each module.
2.3 Prioritize the features.
2.4 Create web page skeleton.
2.5 Prototype with Axure.
2.6 Communicate with developers and reconfirm details.
2.7 Test and update debug lists.
